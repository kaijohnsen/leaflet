Historical WWII Landmarks

By Kai Johnsen

The 20 locations on this map represent key moments in the history of WWII. Each landmark played a crucial role in shaping the outcome of the war. These locations also honor the lives that were lost and the courage that was displayed in the world's deadliest war. The map highlights various parts of the war and offers a deeper understanding of the war's consequences. View this link for more information on how to use Leaflet and JSON data.
